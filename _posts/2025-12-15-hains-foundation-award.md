location: "Grand Hotel, Lund"

I am honoured to receive 1.75 MSEK from the Jan Hains Foundation for Scientific and Technical Research in the Environment and Climate, in recognition of research with significant impact towards a fossil-free and sustainable society.

The grant funds the project *Hydrogen as a Catalyst for Climate-Neutral Fuels: Advancing Efficiency in the Energy Transition*, in which we will use pore-scale and channel-scale simulations to improve water and gas management in hydrogen energy devices.

I presented the project as a popular science talk, *Hydrogen for Climate-Neutral Fuels: A Strategic Initiative for Sweden's Energy Transition*, at the award ceremony at the Grand Hotel in Lund on 15 December 2025. Many thanks to the foundation for its support.
