This July and August I am a visiting researcher at the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT), continuing our collaboration on conjugate heat transfer in turbulent flows over irregular rough walls.

The visit builds on joint work with Dr. Alexander Stroh's group, including our recent contributions on heat conjugation effects over rough walls (ICHMT 2025) and our upcoming paper at the 14th International Symposium on Turbulence and Shear Flow Phenomena (TSFP) in Heidelberg. Looking forward to a productive summer of simulations and discussions.
