On 1–2 June we welcomed the Swedish Society for Industrial Fluid Mechanics and Heat Transfer (SweSIFH) to Lund for its 3rd national seminar, which I had the pleasure of organizing.

Over two days, researchers and engineers from across Sweden discussed industrial fluid mechanics and heat transfer — from gas turbine cooling to electrification and hydrogen technologies. Thank you to all speakers and participants for making it a lively meeting, and to the Department of Energy Sciences for hosting.
