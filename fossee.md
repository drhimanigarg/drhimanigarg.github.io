# FOSSEE internships

Since 2025, I am a **Faculty Partner of the FOSSEE CFD project** — the *Free/Libre and Open Source Software for Education* initiative run by IIT Bombay and funded by the Ministry of Education, Government of India. FOSSEE promotes the use of open-source tools in science and engineering education, and its CFD team trains students across India in OpenFOAM through case study projects, textbook companions, and lab migrations.

As a Faculty Partner, I mentor interns who develop and validate OpenFOAM case studies in my areas of interest: **non-Newtonian flows, turbulence, multiphase flows, heat transfer, and fuel cells/electrolyzers**.
